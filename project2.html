Project 2

Poster Design

Adobe Illustrator

Project Description: For this assignment, we had to create a movie poster for a film adaptation for one of the short stories. I chose to do "The Secret Life of Walter Mitty" by James Thurber. Using Adobe Illustrator, I created a film poster that I thought represented the short story. I used colorful illustrations that related to the short story. I created a catchy tagline to intrigue the audience. I added my own release date, star actor and director name.
