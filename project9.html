Project 9

Portait Animation

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Animate

Project Description: For this assignment, I created a portrait of me in Adobe Illustrator. Using Adobe Animate, I used frames and created different images in every 2 frames to create an animation of drawings. I even made the portrait of myself move to make it more interesting. I added many different, colorful elements to make it look interesting. I then looped everything to make it fit within the time frame required.
