Project 4

Create a New Species

Adobe Photoshop

Project Description: For this assignment, I created a composite of a new species using Adobe Photoshop. I used different components of different images and created this image. I cut out pieces and used many different tools to join the elements together to make them look connected. I also played with effects and color tones that helped emphasize the image and relate to the time period I thought this species would be from. I added some texture in the background as well.
